A bit about me...

My coding journey began on 13 June 2022 with the help of SheCodes. Having worked in the legal industry for a couple of years, I realised that I didn't have a passion for law. Instead, I craved for a job that would let my creativity flow whilst being able to develop my professional identity.

Listening to Matt Delac's (CEO of SheCodes) free workshop insprired me to try front-end development. It sounded like the perfect balance between design and code. I quickly jumped onto his workshops and gave it a go. So far, I am loving front-end development and I am grateful for Matt's excellent mentorship. I can't wait to see what the future holds for me and become a professional developer one day.
